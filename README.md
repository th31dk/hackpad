# the_idk's Hackpad
This is my submission for hackpad.

## BOM
9 Cherry MX Switches
9 1N4148 Diodes
9 Blank DSA Keycaps
16 SK6812 MINI-E LEDs
1 EC11 Rotary Encoder
1 XIAO RP2040
1 0.91in OLED Display
4 M3x16 Bolt
4 M3 Heatset
